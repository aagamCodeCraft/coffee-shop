Our Story

Passion brewed into every cup

How It All Began

Coffee Haven started in 2015 with a simple idea: create a warm, welcoming space where coffee lovers could enjoy exceptional coffee in a comfortable environment.

Founded by two friends who shared a passion for coffee, our first small shop quickly became a neighborhood favorite. We've grown since those early days, but our commitment to quality and community remains unchanged.
